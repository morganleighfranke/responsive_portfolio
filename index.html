About Me

My background is in sales, sales training, and sales management. The most rewarding role I've had in sales was consulting for socially minded businesses to increase sales results in East Africa in the sanitation and solar industries. Before working abroad I worked at Stack Overflow on the sales team for their Careers Platform. My role was to call on CTO's and Hiring Managers to sell them Ad space on the Stack Overflow platform to advertise their job postings. Being immersed in the world of developers I started to learn to code myself and I found that I was energized and intrigued by the conversations I was having about programming.
